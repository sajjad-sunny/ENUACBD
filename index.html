Frequently Asked Questions

To apply for Degree programs, you need to download the admission form from our website, fill it out, and submit it along with the required documents before the deadline. Please visit the Admission section for detailed instructions.

You can check your Honors exam results by visiting the Results section on our website. Select the course and semester, and the results will be displayed. Make sure to have your roll number and registration details ready.

The admission deadline for Masters programs varies by semester. Please refer to the Admission section for the most up-to-date information on admission deadlines and requirements.

Yes, you can request a change of major after admission, but it's subject to availability and approval. Please contact the Academic Affairs department for more information.

You can easily download the academic calendar for the current year from the Academic Calendar & Routine section on our website. It provides important dates for classes, exams, and holidays.

You can find study materials and exam suggestions in the Suggestion & Study Materials section. We offer resources for a wide range of subjects to help you prepare for your exams effectively.
